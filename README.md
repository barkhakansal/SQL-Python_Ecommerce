# 📊 E-commerce Data Analysis Insights
Overview

This project analyzes a real-world e-commerce dataset to extract actionable business insights.
It covers customer behavior, sales trends, revenue patterns, and payment methods, using SQL, Python, and data visualization libraries.

## 🔍 Objective

Understand customer demographics and purchase behavior.

Identify top-selling products and categories.

Analyze revenue trends over time.

Explore payment methods and installment patterns.

Provide recommendations to improve sales and customer satisfaction.

## 🗂 Dataset

The dataset includes:

customers – customer details (city, state, ZIP code).

orders – order timestamps and status.

order_items – products per order.

products – product categories and details.

payments – payment method, value, and installments.

Data source: Open e-commerce dataset (Kaggle/OLX type dataset).

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

SQL – Data extraction and aggregation.

Python – Data analysis with pandas, numpy.

Matplotlib / Seaborn – Data visualization.

Word/Docx – Report generation (optional).

Jupyter Notebook – Interactive exploration and plotting.

## 📈 Key Insights

Customer Locations:
Most customers are concentrated in a few major cities, which can help target marketing campaigns.

Orders Over Time:
Orders peak during specific months, indicating seasonal demand.

Revenue by Product Category:
Certain categories drive the majority of revenue—focus promotions and inventory accordingly.

Payment Behavior:
A significant portion of orders are paid in installments, highlighting customer preference for flexible payments.

Top Sellers by Revenue:
Identify products contributing most to overall revenue for inventory prioritization.
